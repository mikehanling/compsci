SI 204 Spring 2017

Labs

Overview

We will have a lab every week on Tuesday. These are your best chance to get one-on-one help with programming and to get better at writing code.

The completed lab is due by Midnight the following Monday. Every student is responsible to complete the labs, even if you missed class on the day the lab was assigned.

For labs, collaboration is allowed as long as it is clearly documented and in accordance with the course policy.

Labs

  • Lab 1: Linux intro and binary numbers
  • Lab 2: Currency conversion
  • Lab 3: Books
  • Lab 4: Sports Stats
  • Lab 5: Vegas Baby!
  • Lab 6: Green-Screening
  • Lab 7: Serious Arrays
  • Lab 8: Sorting PCAPs
  • Lab 9: 2D Arrays and Images
  • Lab 10: Linked lists
